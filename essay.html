Essay


Tic Tac Toe is a game that may seem fun and too easy and really is more of a challenge when learning important thinking skills. Sometimes the feel of playing a normal game is tricky which reveals the challenges. The O and X symbols represent another name for the game Tic Tac Toe which is Noughts and Crosses (University of Northern Iowa, n. d.). When we play, we are player X and our opponent is player O. Our opponent could be another player such as a friend if playing manually. Otherwise the computer can be our opponent if the game is automated. The game result for us can be either a win, loss, or a tie depending on how the game is played. Winning a game in Tic Tac Toe requires 3 of a player’s marks in a row in any direction; up, down, across, or diagonally (Exploratorium (Explore), n. d.).

The game is completely over when all 9 squares are occupied. If neither player (X or O) occupies 3 squares in a row, the game results in a tie. Being strategic at Tic Tac Toe focuses on how to get 3 marks in a row as well as trying to prevent our component from getting 3 marks in a row whether another player or the computer. After we place a mark (X) in a square, we should observe where our opponent (O) will choose and plan ahead based on that. It is possible our opponent can place 2 marks in a row and increase a chance of a loss for us. We may have to choose a specific square sometimes to keep the game going from a loss given our opponent has 2 marks in a row. Overall, this game focuses on skills where we pay attention and plan ahead based on conditions we act on and what our opponents give us (Exploratorium (Explore), n. d.).

Children as young as age 3 may play Tic Tac Toe, but often for fun competitively given they are unlikely to abide the 3-in-a-row of X or O in the square grid. While often that is the case, so is the result of both players winning if they both fulfill the 3-in-a-row rule in the grid. Players new to the game may need to be familiar with all possibilities of how to get 3 in a row. If the other player can observe a way to get 3 in a row, it can be very tricky for a new player to see the possibility right away. This tricky situation also applies to blocking approaches, so players may start developing 2-way approaches to try to win. The important skills overall reveal the planning ahead implementation with 2 or more moves.
